# Rowad

Rowad is an integrated online platform that supports entrepreneurs and business owners in starting and growing their projects. The platform helps users explore business locations and receive smart recommendations, while also offering AI-powered interior design suggestions, logo creation services, and financial analysis tools to support business planning and branding.
Rowad delivers these services through a simple and user-friendly digital experience.
